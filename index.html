Privacy Policy – 2048 Zenith

Effective date: 2026-05-02

2048 Zenith ("the App", formerly published as "2048+") is a free puzzle game developed by Trong Pong ("we", "us"). This page describes how the App handles information.

Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device, in the App's private storage:

  • Your high scores and game progress
  • Your settings (audio, haptics, language, accessibility options)
  • Your assigned user ID, username, and friend code (see Online Features)

Online Features

The App offers optional online features powered by Google Firebase Realtime Database (Google LLC). When you submit a score to the global leaderboard, add a friend, or play a challenge, the following data is transmitted to and stored in Firebase:

  • An anonymous user ID generated by the App
  • Your chosen username and friend code
  • Your scores and the game mode they were achieved in
  • Your friend list and challenge records (mode, scores, timestamps)

The App also integrates with Google Play Games Services to obtain a stable player identity on Android. Google may collect data according to its own privacy policy.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (GAID / IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review Google's practices and opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Opt out of personalized ads

Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device is removed when you uninstall the App. Data stored in Firebase (user ID, username, friend code, scores, challenge records) is retained indefinitely so that leaderboards and friend relationships continue to work, until you request deletion (see Your Rights below).

Your Rights (EEA / UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR grant you the following rights regarding personal data we hold about you:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

The lawful basis for processing the online-features data listed above is your consent, given by voluntarily choosing to use the leaderboard, friend, or challenge features. You may withdraw consent at any time by requesting deletion of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please open an issue on our GitHub repository (see Contact). Include your in-app friend code so we can locate the correct record. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. AdMob is configured to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy where applicable.

Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Firebase (online leaderboard, friends, challenges)
  • Google Play Games Services (player identity on Android)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise any of your data rights, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.
